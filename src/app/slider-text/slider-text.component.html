CDB (Cannabidiol):
Considerado el cannabinoide terapéutico debido a sus muchas posibles propiedades beneficiosas (analgésicas, antiinflamatorias, antináuseas y otras) que son tanto efectivas como no intoxicantes. Se necesita más investigación para comprender todo el potencial terapéutico.
CBG (Cannabigerol):
The first cannabinoid ever identified. It has displayed antifungal, antibacterial, antidepressant, and pain-relieving properties–and is non-intoxicating like CBD.
CBN (Cannabinol):
Shown to promote better sleep, help sedate the body, alleviate pain and nausea, and support a sense of calm–with minimal intoxicating effects.
CBC (Cannabichromene):
Studies demonstrate benefits to mood, pain, inflammation, brain cells, and even acne. It is non-intoxicating and aids in the “entourage effect.”
THC (Tetrahydrocannabinol):
Proven to sedate, relax, and relieve. Can also affect cognition, consciousness, mood, and energy level—as it's intoxicating.
